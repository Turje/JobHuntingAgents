How CastNet Works

A multi-agent platform that automates every step of your job search — from discovering companies to drafting personalized outreach.

The Agents

1. Discovery Agent

Takes your natural-language query and finds matching companies using web search and AI reasoning. Identifies companies across industries that match your interests.

2. Research Agent

Deep-dives into each discovered company: engineering culture, R&D approach, hiring signals, tech use cases, funding stage, and more.

3. Skills Agent

Analyzes each company's tech stack and compares it to your skills. Identifies gaps, alignment scores, and what you'd need to learn.

4. Tools Agent

Suggests buildable tools, demos, or products for each company — things you can create to impress hiring managers and demonstrate domain expertise.

5. Contact Agent

Identifies key decision-makers at each company: hiring managers, team leads, and department heads relevant to the role you're targeting.

6. Resume Agent

Tailors your resume for each company, emphasizing the most relevant skills, projects, and experience based on what the company values.

7. Outreach Agent

Drafts personalized cold emails for each contact, incorporating company research, your tailored resume summary, and a clear call-to-action.

8. Export

Compiles all results into a downloadable Excel spreadsheet for offline review and tracking.

Search Modes

Choose a search mode before running your query to focus the agents on the right type of opportunities.

General Mode

  • Searches for any type of role
  • Broad industry and company coverage
  • Generic skill analysis and outreach
  • Best for exploring opportunities widely

DS / ML Mode

  • Targets data science and ML roles specifically
  • Finds DS/ML teams at companies in ANY industry
  • Emphasizes ML frameworks, data tools, and AI infrastructure
  • Contacts data science leadership specifically
